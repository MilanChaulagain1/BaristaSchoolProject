Basic Course

Aims to equip participants with essential skills, from mastering espresso extraction and milk frothing to fostering excellent customer service, ensuring they are well-prepared for a successful entry into the world of coffee preparation and service and help gain insights into the art of coffee presentation.



Intermediate Course

Builds on foundational skills, delving deeper into advanced techniques such as precision grind adjustment, intricate latte art designs, and nuanced flavor profiling, ensuring participants develop a refined expertise and heightened proficiency in the art and science of coffee craftsmanship.



Advanced Course

Covers the advanced topics like single-origin coffee exploration, experimental brewing methods, and in-depth sensory analysis, elevating participants to the pinnacle of their barista journey with an emphasis on innovation, expertise, and a nuanced understanding of the coffee industry.
